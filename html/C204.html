  • MAF 1386-2016 Records created or inherited by the Agriculture, Fisheries and Food Departments, and related bodies

    Records of the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food (MAFF) and the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs (DEFRA), their predecessors, and of related bodies.

    The frequently changing structures of these various ministries have meant that responsibility for the same food product, policy or agricultural development has often moved between several departments. Their functions comprise, or have comprised, the general areas of:

    • Administrative records
    • Agricultural economics
    • Agricultural education and advisory services
    • Agricultural labour, wages and farm safety
    • Agricultural machinery
    • Agricultural training boards
    • Animal health and veterinary services
    • Arable, crops and cereals
    • Commissions and enquiries
    • Defence and civil emergencies
    • Divisional offices
    • Environment and Conservation
    • Fertilisers
    • Fisheries
    • Food divisions
    • Food standards and commercial control
    • Horticulture and seeds
    • Information and Publicity
    • Land drainage and flooding
    • Land Tenure, Enclosure, Copyhold and Tithes, and Land Use and Improvement
    • Legal department
    • Marketing
    • Meat and livestock
    • Milk and milk products
    • Ministry of Food
    • Pests and infestation
    • Poultry, eggs and small livestock
    • Regional and Welsh offices
    • Rural Development Programme for England
    • Scientific advisers
    • Statistics and agricultural reports
    • Trade and international concerns

    For series created for regularly archived websites (not specified here), please see the separate Websites Division.
